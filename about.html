About Us

Our Story

“OneMoreStep was born out of a simple but powerful realization: navigating indoor spaces is something most of us take for granted, but for the visually impaired, it can be a daily struggle. In a conversation with a visually impaired person, he shared his frustration with the “last step”-that final stretch where traditional GPS fails and independence feels out of reach.

Motivated by this heartfelt need, our team of innovators, designers and accessibility advocates embarked on a mission. We imagined a world where a simple NFC tag or voice button would allow the visually impaired to navigate a mall, airport, or hospital as seamlessly as anyone else.

Countless interviews, user feedback sessions, and partnerships with support organizations for the visually impaired have shaped our journey. Each step has deepened our understanding and our commitment to creating solutions that are not only practical but life-changing.

At OneMoreStep, we're more than a team; we're a movement toward inclusion, independence, and innovation. We're just getting started.”

Our Mission

At OneMoreStep, our mission is to empower visually impaired individuals by providing seamless indoor navigation solutions that enhance safety, independence, and accessibility.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone, regardless of visual ability, can navigate spaces with confidence and ease.

Our Value

  • Inclusivity: Making the world more accessible for everyone.
  • Innovation: Pushing boundaries with cutting-edge technology.
  • Empowerment: Enhancing safety and independence through seamless solutions.
